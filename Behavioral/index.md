Ada 10 jenis behavioral design patterns:

1. [Template Method](Template-Method)
2. [Observer](Observer)
3. [State](State)
4. [Strategy](Strategy)
5. [Mediator](Mediator)
6. [Iterator](Iterator)
7. Chain of Responsibility
8. Command
9. Visitor
10. Memento
